RequestState

RequestState is used to hold the four part relationship of an asynchronous request. It is like a four way version of an Either. Instead of Left and Right the RequestState can be either be empty, fetching, an error or a success. These states are represented through the identites:

  • RequestEmpty
  • RequestFetching
  • RequestError
  • RequestSuccess
